Dog Walking

Individual walks (with the exception of multiple dogs in one family).
Tailored to suit your dog’s particular needs.

Our walks are especially suitable for a nervous dog or a dog with behavioural issues. Your dog will benefit from our undivided attention. We can of course continue to use any training commands that the dog has been used to following.

All new dogs are walked on the lead until we are satisfied with their recall. Your dogs will not be walked off lead without your written consent.

We ask that all dogs is wear an ID tag and that they have been identi-chipped by a vet (now UK law as of April 2016). We also ask that your pet is up to date with vaccinations, for the welfare of your dog and others in our care.

We like to send a quick text, photo or email post walk. It’s comforting to know that once settled back at home your dogs will be given fresh water, treats and cuddles!

Pet Visits

These home visits are ideal for all pets: especially puppies and kittens, senior dogs, bitches in season or pets that are unwell or in recovery from surgery. Allowing your dog a toilet break and stretch in the garden or a quick walk around the block.

We’ll also feed your pet, replenish water bowls and offer copious amounts of fuss and play. We can also administer any medications, groom your pet, and clean up any accidental messes!

These visits are also geared towards caring for and feeding cats and any other small pets whilst you are away on holiday. That way they get to keep their routine and home comforts.

Whilst at your home we can also switch on/off lights or the radio, pull curtains, put out rubbish and recycling, water plants/garden etc.

We like to send a quick text, photo or email post when leaving your home. It’s comforting to know that once settled back at home your dogs will be given fresh water, treats and cuddles!
