Hounds of Love

Dog walking and home visits for pets

Welcome!

Hounds of Love is an independent, friendly dog walking and pet care service based in the beautiful Somerset countryside near the city of Bath.


The extraordinary bond we share with our pets is beyond measure. We're on call to provide reliable love and care whether you are out at work, away on a trip or just rather busy.

We are fully insured and DBS (CRB) checked.

Areas covered

Priston, Marksbury, Englishcombe, Inglesbatch, Farmbourgh, Tunley, Dunkerton, Timsbury, Combe Hay, Wellow, Peasdown St. John, Odd Down and Combe Down.

About me

I’m Kirsty! I’m a lifelong animal lover and dog worshipper… In fact people who know me well would say I’m animal nuts! I live with my husband Nick and our 12 year old tripawd rescue dog Roland. He’s a senior with the heart of a puppy, and an absolute legend. As you can see by our header image, he’s also rather photogenic! I’ve grown up with dogs, cats and a host of small furry friends and so it’s always been a major goal of mine to work with animals. After years of working in admin, then a recent life changing house move from the city to rural Somerset, I decided it was time to make another change. Creating my own business, devoting my time to four legged friends, and henceforth Hounds of Love was born!
