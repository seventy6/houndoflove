Services and Prices

Dog Walking

Individual walks (with the exception of multiple dogs in one family).
Tailored to suit your dog’s particular needs.

We prefer to concentrate on individual walks. This way your dog will benefit from our undivided attention. Our walks are generally 1-1 but we're also happy to accommodate extra dogs if they already live in the same household or are good buddies and used to being walked together. 1-1 walks are especially suitable for dogs with a nervous disposition or rescue dogs with behavioural issues. We can of course continue to use any ongoing training that the dog has been used to following.

All new dogs are walked on the lead until we are satisfied with their recall. Your dogs will not be walked off lead without your written consent.

We ask that all dogs is wear an ID tag and that they have been identi-chipped by a vet (now UK law as of April 2016). We also ask that your pet is up to date with vaccinations, for the welfare of your dog and others in our care.

Pet Visits

These home visits are especially perfect for small animals, cats, puppies and kittens, and pets that are older, unwell or in recovery from surgery. Whether you need somebody to pop in to let your dog out for a toilet break, feed and replenish water bowls or you may require a longer visit, providing company, playtime and extra cuddles for your pet... we can be there to put your mind at rest.

We can also administer any medications, groom your pet, or clean up litter trays or any accidental messes!

Whether you are out for the day or away on holiday, these sessions ensure your beloved animals get to keep to their general routine and home comforts.

--------

Whilst at your home we can also switch on/off lights or the radio, pull curtains, put out rubbish and recycling, water plants/garden etc.
